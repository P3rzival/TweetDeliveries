About us

Spicy Slice is a pizza restaurant, established in December 2016 by the infamous Team 13 - Borja Leyva, Ovidiu Grec, Zhikang He, Alex Wood, Robert Winslow, Hyun Han and Peter Bull. Here we have a passion for making every pizza just as great as the last! Currently operating in the Sheffield area, we are looking to expand out in the future and allow as many people as possible to taste our fantastic food!
